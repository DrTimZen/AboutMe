about me

I'm a Berlin based pedagogue who worked at social services for nearly 20 years. Over the time I fell in love with media design and digital tools. I taught myself Adobe CS especially InDesign, Photoshop and Illustrator, coding websites via HTML and CSS and created layouts and designs on part-time projects. Later I practiced my HTML and CSS skills and got started with JavaScript via freecodecamp, Udemy, codewars and other sources.

The last two years I worked for Inklusion-Digital GmbH in Berlin. We made a webapp called SPLINT for teachers simpflying aid planing and teaching of students with special needs. First I was responsible for pedagogical content and later for building a sales team, managing workshops and product presentations and lot more smaller and greater things.

I love to share my passion for digital tools with people (especially teachers and other pedagogues) and help them learning new applications and the everyday usage. Important for me is an easy, fun bringing and emphatic way of communication at eye level. I am always open for job offers, projects and new people in generell.
